◆ About The Pulse Project

"We already know people are suffering.
We exist to ask why that's still allowed to happen."

The Pulse Project is a student-led research publication focused on health outcomes in conflict zones, refugee and displaced communities, disaster-affected populations, and remote underserved areas. Founded at Folsom Lake College, it exists to bridge the gap between what we know and what actually changes on the ground.
